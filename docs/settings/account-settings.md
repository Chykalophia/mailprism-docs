# Account Settings

Account settings control your personal MailPrism experience. These settings follow you across workspaces and are separate from workspace settings.

## Profile

### Basic Information

Update your profile details:

1. Go to **Account** → **Profile**
2. Edit your information:
   - **Display Name** — How you appear in the app
   - **Email Address** — Your login email
   - **Avatar** — Upload a profile picture
   - **Timezone** — For accurate scheduling and timestamps

### Changing Your Email

To change your login email:

1. Go to **Account** → **Profile**
2. Click **Change Email**
3. Enter your new email address
4. Verify via the confirmation link sent to your new email
5. Your old email will receive a notification

:::warning
Changing your email updates your login credentials. Make sure you have access to the new email address.
:::

## Security

### Password

If you signed up with email/password:

1. Go to **Account** → **Security**
2. Click **Change Password**
3. Enter your current password
4. Enter and confirm your new password
5. Click **Update Password**

**Password Requirements:**
- Minimum 8 characters
- At least one uppercase letter
- At least one number
- At least one special character

### Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Add an extra layer of security:

1. Go to **Account** → **Security**
2. Find **Two-Factor Authentication**
3. Click **Enable 2FA**
4. Choose your method:
   - **Authenticator App** (recommended) — Google Authenticator, Authy, etc.
   - **SMS** — Text message codes
5. Follow the setup instructions
6. Save your backup codes securely

### Active Sessions

View and manage where you're logged in:

1. Go to **Account** → **Security**
2. Find **Active Sessions**
3. See all devices and browsers with active sessions
4. Click **Revoke** to log out of any session

### Login History

Review recent login activity:

- Date and time
- Device and browser
- IP address and location
- Login status (success/failure)

## Notifications

### Email Notifications

Control what emails you receive from MailPrism:

| Notification | Description | Default |
|--------------|-------------|---------|
| **Weekly Summary** | Automation stats and insights | On |
| **Rule Alerts** | When rules fail or need attention | On |
| **VIP Emails** | When VIP contacts email you | Off |
| **Product Updates** | New features and improvements | On |
| **Tips & Tutorials** | Help getting more from MailPrism | On |

### In-App Notifications

Configure notifications within the app:

- **Desktop notifications** — Browser push notifications
- **Sound alerts** — Audio notification for important events
- **Badge count** — Show unread count on browser tab

### Notification Schedule

Set quiet hours:

1. Go to **Account** → **Notifications**
2. Enable **Quiet Hours**
3. Set start and end times
4. Notifications are silenced during these hours

## Privacy

### Data & Privacy

Control your personal data:

- **Download My Data** — Export all your MailPrism data
- **Delete Account** — Permanently delete your account and data

### Data Export

Request a full export of your data:

1. Go to **Account** → **Privacy**
2. Click **Download My Data**
3. We'll prepare your export (may take up to 24 hours)
4. Download link will be sent to your email

Export includes:
- Account information
- Rules and configurations
- Activity logs
- Contact lists
- Templates

### Account Deletion

To permanently delete your account:

1. Go to **Account** → **Privacy**
2. Click **Delete Account**
3. Enter your password to confirm
4. Type "DELETE" to verify
5. Click **Permanently Delete**

:::danger
Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data, rules, and settings will be permanently removed.
:::

## Preferences

### Display Settings

Customize how MailPrism looks:

- **Theme** — Light, Dark, or System
- **Emails per page** — 25, 50, or 100
- **Compact mode** — Denser interface layout
- **Sender display** — Email, name, or both

### Date & Time

Configure date and time formats:

- **Date format** — MM/DD/YYYY, DD/MM/YYYY, YYYY-MM-DD
- **Time format** — 12-hour or 24-hour
- **Week starts on** — Sunday or Monday
- **Timezone** — Auto-detect or manual selection

### Language

Currently, MailPrism is available in English. More languages coming soon.

## Billing (Personal)

If you have a personal subscription:

### View Subscription

See your current plan:

- Plan name and tier
- Billing cycle (monthly/annual)
- Next billing date
- Usage statistics

### Manage Payment

Update payment methods:

1. Go to **Account** → **Billing**
2. Click **Manage Payment**
3. Add, update, or remove payment methods
4. Set default payment method

### Billing History

View past invoices:

- Download PDF invoices
- View payment history
- See upcoming charges

### Change Plan

Upgrade or downgrade your subscription:

1. Go to **Account** → **Billing**
2. Click **Change Plan**
3. Select your new plan
4. Review changes and confirm

:::info
For workspace/team billing, see [Workspace Settings](/settings/workspace-settings).
:::
