## Why MailPrism?

### Save Hours Every Week

The average professional spends **2.5 hours per day** on email. MailPrism automates the repetitive tasks so you can reclaim your time.

### Intelligent, Not Just Automatic

Unlike simple email filters, MailPrism uses AI to understand the *meaning* behind your emails. It can detect urgency, identify important senders, and make nuanced decisions that simple keyword matching can't.

### Your Privacy Matters

MailPrism processes your emails securely and never shares your data with third parties. Your inbox stays yours.
