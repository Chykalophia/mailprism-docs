## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes! Upgrade instantly and downgrade at the end of your billing period.

### Is there a free trial?

The Free tier is always available. For paid features, we offer a 14-day money-back guarantee.

### What happens if I exceed my limits?

You'll receive a notification and can upgrade immediately. Rules continue working until you significantly exceed limits.

### Do annual plans include all features?

Yes, annual plans include the same features as monthly plans with a 20% discount.

### Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, no long-term contracts. Cancel anytime from your account settings.

### Do you offer discounts for nonprofits or education?

Yes! Contact us for special pricing for qualified organizations.
